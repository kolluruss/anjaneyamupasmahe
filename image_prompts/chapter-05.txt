The background features ancient temple structures and a sky filled with "Paramananda" (Supreme Bliss) and falling lotus petals. The atmosphere depicts profound inner joy, divine intervention, and the timeless richness of Hindu spiritual culture. Art style: highly detailed, soulful, traditional Indian aesthetic, with vibrant, holy lighting and a sense of infinite tranquility.
