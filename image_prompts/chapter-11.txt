A serene and miraculous scene in the sacred Naimisharanya forest. A humble hunter (Gala), previously looking frail, is now radiant and standing in deep prayer before Sage Pushkara. The sage is sprinkling holy water (Padodaka) on him. In the background, Lord Hanuman and his consort Suvarchala appear in a glowing golden orb of light, bestowing health and peace. The atmosphere is lush, with deer and ancient banyan trees, promoting profound inner joy.
