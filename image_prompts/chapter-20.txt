The grand finale of spiritual attainment. Sage Kapila is in deep Samadhi on a riverbank. Lord Hanuman and Suvarchala Devi appear before him, seated on a thousand-petaled celestial lotus. Hanuman is four-armed, holding sacred symbols, radiating a light that rivals a thousand suns. The entire scene is bathed in "Paramananda" (Supreme Bliss), with celestial beings throwing flowers from the clouds, signifying the ultimate success of the Shodasharnava mantra.
