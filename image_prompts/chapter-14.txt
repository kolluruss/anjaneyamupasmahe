A celebratory and heartwarming scene showing a miraculous transformation. Dhvajadatta returns to find his simple hut replaced by a magnificent, gem-studded palace, glowing under Hanuman's grace. His wife and children are dressed in fine silks, their faces filled with pure joy. A small, permanent golden shrine of Hanuman is at the center of their home, radiating protective light. The scene depicts ancient Hindu prosperity and the reward of unwavering faith.
