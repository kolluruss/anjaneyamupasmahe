A celestial concert scene. Lord Hanuman is singing with closed eyes, radiating a golden glow. Around him, two beautiful celestial lutes (Veenas) are partially submerged in a large rock that has turned into shimmering, liquid water. Naga Kanyas (divine serpent maidens) are watching from the sides with folded hands, their faces filled with "Paramananda." The scene is set in a glowing cave with ancient crystals.
