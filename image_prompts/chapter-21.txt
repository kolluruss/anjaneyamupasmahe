A serene cinematic scene on the misty banks of the Ganges (Bhagirathi). A thin but radiant Brahmin (Kapila) is receiving initiation from a luminous Sage. The Sage is placing his hand on Kapila's head. In the background, the river flows peacefully under a golden sunrise. A subtle, giant shadow of Hanuman appears in the clouds, symbolizing protection. Atmosphere: "Paramananda" and divine hope.
