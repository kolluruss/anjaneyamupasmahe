An epic yet serene scene from the Mahabharata. The grand chariot of Arjuna, driven by Krishna, features a vibrant banner with a powerful image of Lord Hanuman (Kapi-dhvaja). Hanuman himself is seen in a subtle, glowing form seated atop the banner, his eyes fixed on the horizon, creating an impenetrable shield of golden energy around the chariot. The battlefield is in the distance, but the focus is on the divine protection and the calm confidence it brings.
