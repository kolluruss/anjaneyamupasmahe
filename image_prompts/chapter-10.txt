The ultimate moment of divine realization: Lord Hanuman and his consort Suvarchala Devi appear in a burst of brilliant golden and silvery light before the devotee Dhvajadatta. Hanuman is adorned in yellow silks and golden ornaments, while Suvarchala radiates a soft, cooling light like the moon. They are seated together on a celestial lotus, bestowing supreme bliss and prosperity. The scene is a masterpiece of Hindu spiritual art, promoting absolute inner joy, peace, and the beauty of divine union.
