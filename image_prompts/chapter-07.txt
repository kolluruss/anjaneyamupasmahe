A cosmic and grand cinematic scene where Lord Hanuman extends his massive, glowing tail over a thousand miles across a starry sky to form a divine bridge. Within the hairs of his tail, planets and stars twinkle like dewdrops on grass. On this celestial path, a fierce yet divine form of Sita (as Kali) is supported by Hanuman's five-faced power. The atmosphere is epic yet serene, depicting the triumph of light over darkness amidst a backdrop of nebulae and ancient constellations.
